About Me

My name is Nicole Maneth. I am a Junior studying Interdisciplinary Computing with an emphasis in Astronomy at the University of Kansas. I have interests in mobile and web development. I am also interested in opportunities that allow me to combine science with computing.

I am the President of Dennis E. Rieger Scholarship Hall. I am also a member of Theta Tau Professional Engineering Fraternity where I have served as Web, Bylaw, and Professional Development Chairs.

I run a craft blog at called Nicole Creates. I also have an Etsy shop where I sell handmade crochet items. I have a YouTube channel where I post music and educational content.
