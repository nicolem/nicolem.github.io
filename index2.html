BK 411

The directory of students or known as the Kelley Calling around school.

The Kelley Calling, BK 411, is an iOS app deesigned to replace the print versions of the school directory handed out to the student at the beginning of the year. This app gives the students access to all the same info, plus they can directly call, text or email from inside the app. The student info is just JSON parsed into a manipulatable form for the students to use. This is not currenty avialable to people outside the Kelley community, but you can still download it or my first app I created which was also for my alma matter by following the links below.

Storgy

A Ruby on Rails app for collaborative story telling

Storgy is a project from this past summer of 2014, for a coworker at my internship. Storgy is a site for creating and reading stories written by multiple induviduals. Anyone can login via Twitter and contribute up to 50 words to any story started, or start their own story in 50 words and watch it grow.
